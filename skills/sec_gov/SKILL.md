# SKILL: SEC Statutes & Regulations Downloader

## Target
URL: https://www.sec.gov/rules-regulations/statutes-regulations

## Goal
Download the major SEC regulation PDF files from the page above and save them to the downloads directory.

## Step-by-step Instructions

1. Navigate to the URL above.

2. The page contains a list of statutes and regulations. Look for links to PDF files. They are typically labeled things like:
   - "Securities Act of 1933"
   - "Securities Exchange Act of 1934"
   - "Investment Advisers Act of 1940"
   - "Investment Company Act of 1940"
   - "Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002"

3. For each PDF link you find, click it to download the file. The files may open in a new tab or trigger a direct download.

4. If a PDF opens in a browser tab rather than downloading, use the browser's save/download function to save it.

5. Download at least 3 PDF files. Priority order:
   - Securities Act of 1933
   - Securities Exchange Act of 1934
   - Investment Advisers Act of 1940

6. If a link leads to an intermediate page (e.g., a document index), navigate into it and find the PDF download link there.

7. If any popup or cookie banner appears, dismiss it before proceeding.

## Expected Output
Multiple PDF files saved to the designated downloads directory.

## Notes
- If a PDF link is broken or returns a 404, skip it and move to the next one.
- Do not download ZIP files, only PDFs.
- The page layout may change over time — use your judgment to find PDF links even if they are in a different location than described.
